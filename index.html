All About Me!

Hello! My name is Kara A. Matuszewski and I am a Full Stack MERN Web Developer currently enrolled in MIT's xPRO Professional Certificate Program.

Prior to my enrollment in the MIT xPro Professional Certificate Program, I have been involved in Product Design, designing usable EMR systems and interfaces for healthcare companies. Over the past couple of months I have become increasingly passionate about making beautiful, functional designs for end users to enjoy. I want to apply my newly learned skills to my web developments as I continue to learn more and more about web development design. As part of my new coding career and aspirations I find new opportunities to combine creativity, problem-solving and logic to develop unique applications.
